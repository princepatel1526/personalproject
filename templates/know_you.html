How well do I know you?

A small pause before our journey continues.

Your name… Mansi Shukla… is not just a name to me.

It’s something that instantly grabs my attention, no matter what I’m doing.

Your roots are beautifully spread.

Madhya Pradesh from your mother’s side, Uttar Pradesh from your father’s… a mix that makes you uniquely you.

We share more than just conversations.

We even come from the same school… maybe that’s why it feels so familiar talking to you.

You don’t chase loudness.

You like gray—simple, calm, and quietly beautiful… just like your personality at times.

You notice beauty in soft things.

Like lilies… not flashy, but elegant and meaningful.

Your music taste says a lot about you.

Arijit Singh… which means you understand emotions more deeply than you show.

And then there’s your comfort world…

Music, books, and those long naps—you know how to slow down in a fast world.

What is something about you that I don’t know yet?
